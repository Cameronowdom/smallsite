American Teen

American Teen is the debut studio album by Khalid It was released on March 3, 2017, by Right Hand Music Group and RCA Records. The album was supported by the singles "Location", "Young Dumb & Broke" and "Saved". In October 2017, the album was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. The album received a Grammy Award nomination for Best Urban Contemporary Album while its lead single "Location" was nominated for Best R&B Song at the 60th Annual Grammy Awards. In August 2018, the album was certified double platinum by the RIAA.

Suncity

Suncity is the first extended play by Khalid. It was released on October 19, 2018 by RCA Records. It was supported by the single "Better". The EP debuted at number eight on the Billboard 200. The EP was supported by the single "Better", which was released on September 14, 2018. The song went on to reach the top ten in Australia, Belgium, New Zealand and Norway. It peaked at number 8 on the Billboard Hot 100.

Free Spirit

Free Spirit is the second studio album by Khalid, released on April 5, 2019, through RCA Records. It is the follow-up to his 2018 EP Suncity, and includes the single "Talk". Khalid also announced a short film directed by Emil Nava to accompany the album, which was released on April 3. The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200. The album also includes the singles "Better" and "Saturday Nights" from Khalid's 2018 EP Suncity. The song "Outta My Head" peaked at number fifty-eight on the Billboard Hot 100.
